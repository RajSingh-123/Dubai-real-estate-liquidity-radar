# 🏙 Dubai Real Estate Liquidity & Capital Efficiency Radar

## 📌 Overview

The **Dubai Real Estate Liquidity & Capital Efficiency Radar** is an institutional-style investment intelligence model built using Dubai Land Department (DLD) transactional data.

This project moves beyond descriptive dashboards and focuses on:

- Liquidity diagnostics  
- Capital rotation detection  
- Structural volatility assessment  
- Strategic capital allocation signaling  

The model simulates how professional investors evaluate transaction depth, capital efficiency, and market regime shifts across Dubai real estate zones.

---

## 🎯 Project Objective

To identify:

- High-liquidity zones
- Capital-efficient submarkets
- Early rotation signals
- Structural risk clusters

And translate those signals into a rule-based capital deployment framework.

---

## 🧩 Data Source

- Dubai Land Department (DLD) Transactions  
- Monthly aggregated transaction data  
- Lag-adjusted analysis  
- Off-market transactions excluded

## 📊 Core Metrics & Business Logic

| Metric | Simplified Logic | Investment Interpretation |
|--------|------------------|--------------------------|
| Liquidity Ratio | Transactions / Total Market Value | Measures exit depth & turnover strength |
| Exit Velocity Index (EVI) | Change in Transaction Momentum | Detects rotation speed |
| Capital Efficiency | Price Growth ÷ Liquidity Growth | Tests if appreciation is supported by demand |
| Volatility Index | Std Dev of Price Trend | Identifies structural instability |
| Capital at Risk | Volatility × Market Exposure | Estimates downside sensitivity |

---

## 📐 Capital Deployment Framework

**Allocate When:**
- Liquidity Ratio > 2%
- Capital Efficiency above market median
- 3-month momentum positive
- Volatility stable

**Avoid When:**
- Price accelerates without liquidity backing
- Volatility spikes alongside declining transaction depth
- Exit velocity weakens across multiple zones
